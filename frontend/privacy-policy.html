Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2023

Infinite ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is processed in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa.

1. Information We Collect

In order to provide micro-lending services and comply with FICA/KYC regulations, we collect:

  • Personal Identifiers: Full name, SA ID Number, and Date of Birth.
  • Contact Information: Physical address, email, and mobile number.
  • Financial Information: 3-month bank statements and income details.
  • Student Verification: Student ID, registration proof, and bursary details.
  • Biometric Data: Selfies and ID photos for identity verification.

2. Purpose of Processing

Your data is collected for the following purposes:

  • To verify your identity under FICA.
  • To perform affordability assessments as required by the National Credit Act (NCA).
  • To report to Credit Bureaus regarding your repayment history.
  • To communicate with your Guarantor/Co-signer if necessary.

3. Data Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Registered Credit Bureaus (Experian, TransUnion, etc.).
  • Law enforcement or the Sheriff of the Court in the event of default.
  • Our professional advisory team (Attorney and Accountant).

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard SSL encryption and secure server protocols to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or destruction.

5. Your Rights

Under POPIA, you have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal information. To exercise these rights, please contact our Compliance Officer.
