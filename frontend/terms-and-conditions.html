Terms and Conditions

By applying for a loan with Infinity Loans, you (the Borrower) enter into a legally binding agreement governed by the laws of the Republic of South Africa.

1. Interest and Fees

The lending model is structured as follows:

  • Interest Rate: 30% for amounts below R1,000 and 40% for amounts above R1,000.
  • Service Fee: A non-refundable 2% service fee is applied to the principal amount of all loans.

2. Repayment Obligations

Repayments must be made on or before the agreed-upon date. It is the Borrower's responsibility to ensure that funds are available in the designated bank account for collection.

3. Late Payments and Penalties

If a payment is missed or delayed:

  • An immediate Late Payment Penalty of R250 will be charged.
  • Interest will continue to accrue on the outstanding balance.
  • The Borrower will be liable for all collection costs and legal fees on an attorney-and-client scale.
LEGAL NOTICE: Defaulting on this loan will result in immediate reporting to all Credit Bureaus (Blacklisting). We reserve the right to obtain a Court Judgment and instruct the Sheriff of the Court to seize your assets or apply for a Garnishee Order against your salary.

4. Guarantor Liability

The Guarantor/Co-signer is jointly and severally liable for the debt. If the Borrower fails to pay, Infinity Loans will pursue the full outstanding amount from the Guarantor without further notice.

5. FICA Compliance

We reserve the right to refuse any application that does not meet the Know Your Customer (KYC) requirements. Providing fraudulent documents is a criminal offense and will be reported to SAPS.

6. Jurisdiction

This agreement is subject to the jurisdiction of the Magistrate's Court of South Africa.
